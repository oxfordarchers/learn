## The Recurve Bow > A recurve bow is a bow with limbs that curve away from the archer when unstrung. A recurve bow stores more energy and delivers energy more efficiently than an equivalent straight-limbed bow, giving a greater amount of energy and speed to the arrow. - [_Wikipedia_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Recurve_bow)

Scoring

The target face has 10 scoring zones:

  • Gold: 10 and 9
  • (The centre of the 10 is called an "X" and it's still worth 10!)
  • Red: 8 and 7
  • Blue: 6 and 5
  • Black: 4 and 3
  • White: 2 and 1
